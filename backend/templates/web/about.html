About Us

offRez is a listings platform that helps students, renters, buyers and travelers discover places and services faster.

What we do

We connect users with verified listings across multiple categories, with a simple flow to browse, compare, and view details. For student accommodation, we also support university-based browsing so you can quickly find places around your campus.

Our offerings
  • Student accommodation: browse by university, filter by room sharing and gender preferences, and see campus distance where available.
  • Long term: houses and apartments for monthly rent.
  • Short term: day/night stays for travel and visits.
  • Real estate: properties for sale and investment opportunities.
  • Resorts: getaways, lodges and leisure stays.
  • Shops: shop listings and categories where available.
Our goal

Make it faster and safer to find what fits your needs, while giving landlords and businesses an easy way to reach customers.

How it works
  • Browse listings and open a property to see details, photos, and location information (where available).
  • Use filters like price, amenities, and sorting to find the best match.
  • For student accommodation, follow the university flow to focus on your campus area.
